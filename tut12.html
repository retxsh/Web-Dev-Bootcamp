What are Semantic Elements? A semantic element describes its meaning to both the browser and the developer.
- here we have used <summary> tag inside <details> tag.

Some other Semantic Elements are as follows:

  • <article>
  • <figcaption>
  • <footer>
  • <section>
  • <time>
  • <header>
