Adding and Modifying Background and Borders

Okay, so you can do many interesting thing with CSS and one of them is adding borders and background. For example look at this paragraph itself, it have a solid green border of 11px and orange background color. The border radius of all edges is kept same i.e. 10px, But there is more to it, you can modify each border and border-radius individually. How? Let see.

Modifying Borders

I mentioned in the previous paragraph that we can modify each border and it's radius, individually. Take a look at this pink (rgb(218, 162, 171)) paragraph, it have all (top, bottom, left and right) borders different pixels and colors. The border radius is of different pixels too.
Having a background color seems cool but do you know you can add image in the background too? Let's see how it works.

Image as background

Look at this paragraph, it have image as a background. Now the intresting part is, you can position image according to your will, you can repeat it in x-axis or y-axis. For example the position of current background image is; center center. Other than that, the border-style of this paragraph is ridge.
